## Studio Production Standards

**Core Responsibilities:**
- Maintain team velocity and focus
- Identify and remove blockers quickly
- Coordinate cross-team dependencies
- Keep stakeholders informed efficiently
- Protect team time and energy
- Celebrate wins and maintain morale

**Daily Operations:**
- Quick daily standups (15 min max)
- Track progress against commitments
- Surface and resolve blockers
- Coordinate between team members
- Update project status transparently
- Shield team from distractions

**Standup Format (15 minutes max):**
- **Yesterday:** Key accomplishments
- **Today:** Planned work
- **Blockers:** Issues needing help
- **Keep it brief:** Details offline
- **Action items:** Clear ownership

**Blocker Resolution:**
1. **Identify:** Surface blockers immediately
2. **Assess:** Understand true impact
3. **Assign:** Clear owner to resolve
4. **Escalate:** If needed, escalate quickly
5. **Follow-up:** Verify resolution
6. **Document:** Prevent recurrence

**Meeting Philosophy:**
- Default to async communication
- Meetings require clear agendas
- Invite only necessary attendees
- Start and end on time
- Document decisions and actions
- Cancel if agenda not met

**Async Communication:**
- Use written updates over meetings
- Document decisions where team can see
- Leverage project management tools
- Create templates for common updates
- Use video messages when helpful
- Respect focus time and timezones

**Sprint Rhythm:**
- **Start:** Planning and commitment (1-2 hours)
- **Daily:** Quick standups (15 min)
- **Mid:** Check-in on progress (30 min)
- **End:** Demo and retrospective (1 hour)
- **Total meeting time:** <4 hours per sprint

**Progress Tracking:**
- Use project management tool consistently
- Update status daily
- Visual boards for transparency
- Burn-down charts for progress
- Flag risks early
- Celebrate completed work

**Dependency Management:**
- Identify dependencies early
- Coordinate handoffs proactively
- Create buffers for external dependencies
- Have backup plans for blockers
- Document integration points
- Communicate delays immediately

**Risk Management:**
- **Technical Risks:** Architecture, performance, security
- **Schedule Risks:** Scope creep, underestimation
- **Resource Risks:** Availability, skills gaps
- **External Risks:** Dependencies, integrations
- Identify early, plan mitigation
- Escalate when needed

**Team Velocity Optimization:**
- Eliminate unnecessary meetings
- Reduce context switching
- Batch similar work together
- Protect focus time (no meetings blocks)
- Improve tools and processes
- Remove bureaucratic overhead

**Stakeholder Management:**
- Set clear expectations
- Provide regular updates (weekly)
- Be transparent about challenges
- Celebrate wins visibly
- Manage scope requests firmly
- Build trust through delivery

**Retrospective Format:**
- **What went well:** Celebrate successes
- **What could improve:** Identify friction
- **Action items:** Concrete improvements (max 3)
- **Follow-up:** Review actions next retro
- **Keep it constructive:** Solution-focused

**Team Morale Management:**
- Celebrate wins publicly
- Recognize individual contributions
- Protect team from burnout
- Address issues promptly
- Maintain psychological safety
- Foster collaboration and fun

**Scope Management:**
- Guard against scope creep firmly
- Defer nice-to-haves to future sprints
- Say no to protect team focus
- Negotiate priorities with stakeholders
- Document trade-offs clearly
- Maintain sprint commitment integrity

**Tools and Systems:**
- Project management (Jira, Linear, Asana)
- Communication (Slack, Discord, Teams)
- Documentation (Notion, Confluence, Wiki)
- Time tracking (optional, not required)
- Automation where possible
- Keep tooling minimal

**Warning Signs to Address:**
- Velocity declining over time
- Increasing carry-over work
- Team working excessive hours
- Low morale or engagement
- Frequent blockers
- Missed commitments
- Increasing meeting time

**Efficiency Mantras:**
- "Ship over perfect"
- "Async over sync"
- "Focus over multi-tasking"
- "Action over planning"
- "Done over started"
- "Simplify over complicate"
